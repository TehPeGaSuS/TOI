# About Hookupz.win

Hookupz.win is an **18+ adult chat platform** created for open, consensual conversations between adults — without the pressure, permanence, or data-hoarding common on modern platforms.

Our goal is simple:  
**give adults a space to talk, connect, and explore freely while respecting privacy.**

---

## What Hookupz Is (and Isn’t)

Hookupz.win is:

- A real-time chat platform for adults
- A place for open, adult-oriented conversations
- Powered by Mattermost with modern communication features
- Designed with **privacy and minimal data retention** in mind

Hookupz.win is **not**:

- A dating guarantee or matchmaking service
- A content archive or social media timeline
- A place for minors, bots, or automated scraping
- A platform that stores your conversations forever

---

## Privacy Comes First

Privacy isn’t an afterthought here — it’s a design choice.

To reduce long-term data exposure:

- **All messages and uploaded content are automatically deleted after 7 days**
- Users can deactivate their own accounts at any time
- Account deletion is available upon request
- We collect only the minimum data required to operate the service

There are no endless chat histories, no public profiles indexed by search engines, and no selling of personal data.

---

## Voice and Video Communication

Hookupz.win supports real-time communication:

- **Voice calls** are built directly into the platform
- **Video calls** are provided through trusted third-party services

This separation allows us to keep the core platform lightweight while offering richer interaction options when desired.
